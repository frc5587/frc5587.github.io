Our Summer STEM Programs

Titan Robotics is offering a variety of STEM themed programs for students from kindergarten through eighth grade. All of our programs feature content developed and taught by team members and are held at T.C. Williams Highschool.

To register for our programs, complete and send the registration form to frc5587@gmail.com. All programs start at $215 per week except FTC workshops which are free! Payments can be made by mailing a check made out to Titan Robotics 5587 Boosters to 121 Longview Dr, Alexandria, VA, 22314.

To apply for available scholarships, complete and attach the scholarship application form to your child's registration email.

FTC Workshop:

Get Robotics Ready!

Grades 6-12

July 29-August 2
1PM - 3PM
Middle and high school students interested in robotics should attend this awesome workshop at no cost! Create and program a robot to complete a challenge and learn all about how the teams work. This is a great experience, especially for those considering joining the Titan Robotics family in the fall.

Physics FUNdamentals

Grades K-2

July 29-August 2
9AM-12PM
Explore STEM by creating lava lamps, constellations, mini rockets, and more! Campers will learn about the engineering design process through interactive exploration and fun learning. Take home your own crystals and constellations as well as a love for STEM!

Lego Mindstorms

Grades 3-5

July 15-19
9AM-12PM
Campers will work in teams to build and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges. Campers will also learn the engineering design process. This camp is great preparation for FIRST LEGO League!

Rocketry

Grades 3-5

July 15-19
1PM-4PM
Titan Robotics Rocketry camp is out of this world! Campers will have the opportunity to learn about the science of rockets and space travel through interactive lessons. They will also design and build their own rockets which will be launched at the end of the week!

Intro to Java

Grades 6-8

July 22-26
9AM-12PM
Go from zero to hero in Java programming! In this course, campers will go from the basics of computer programming in the popular Java language to intermediate level design in one week. Campers will learn through engaging projects and guidance, ending the week with a project of their own invention and skills to kick off a journey into the world of computer science.

Product and Web Design

Grades 6-8

July 22-26
1PM-4PM
Campers will invent and develop a product of their own design. They will learn to create a model of this product using CAD (computer-aided design) and 3D printing. Then, they will create a web page to showcase their product using basic HTML. This camp is a fun way to utilize STEM skills to learn about the entire product design, development, and marketing process!
