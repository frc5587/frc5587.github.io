Our Summer STEM Programs

Titan Robotics is offering a variety of STEM-themed programs for students from kindergarten through eighth grade. All of our programs feature content developed and taught by team members and are held at T.C. Williams Highschool.

To register for our camps, complete and send the registration form to frc5587@gmail.com. To register for the FTC workshop, all you have to do is fill out this online form. All programs start at $215 per week except for the FTC workshop which is free! Payments can be made by mailing a check made out to Titan Robotics 5587 Boosters to 121 Longview Dr, Alexandria, VA, 22314.

To apply for available scholarships, complete and attach the scholarship application form to your child's registration email. Scholarships are provided by ARServices.

Lego Mindstorms

Grades 3-5

July 15-19
9AM-12PM
Campers will work in teams to build and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges. Campers will also learn the engineering design process. This camp is great preparation for FIRST LEGO League!

Rocketry

Grades 3-5

July 15-19
1PM-4PM
The Titan Robotics Rocketry camp is out of this world! Campers will have the opportunity to learn about the science of rockets and space travel through interactive lessons. They will also design and build their own rockets which will be launched at the end of the week!

Intro to Java

Grades 6-8

July 22-26
9AM-12PM
Go from zero to hero in Java programming! In this course, campers will go from the basics of computer programming in the popular Java language to intermediate level design in one week. Campers will learn through engaging projects and guidance, ending the week with a project of their own invention and skills to kick off a journey into the world of computer science.

Product and Web Design

Grades 6-8

July 22-26
1PM-4PM
Campers will invent and develop a product of their own design. They will learn to create a model of this product using computer-aided design and 3D printing, then write the code for a web page to showcase their product. This camp is a fun way to utilize STEM skills while learning about the entire product design, development, and marketing process!

Physics FUNdamentals

Grades K-2

July 29-August 2
9AM-12PM
Explore STEM by creating lava lamps, constellations, mini rockets, and more! Campers will learn about the engineering design process through interactive exploration and fun learning. Take home your own crystals and constellations as well as a love for STEM!
