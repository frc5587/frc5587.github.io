FRC Team 5587 gives back to our community and promotes STEM awareness through events in the classroom and in the community.

Support Us

Who We Are

Titan Robotics, FRC Team 5587, is a student-run FIRST robotics team from TC Williams High School in Alexandria, Virginia.

About Our School

What We Do

Titan Robotics builds robots each year to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition. In addition, we promote STEM awareness in our school and community by conducting outreach events and supporting robotics teams for younger children.

About FIRST

Why We Do It

By arming students with knowledge and engaging with our community, we not only ensure the longevity of our team, but are able to give back to our community for their support of STEM opportunities and hands-on learning.

Outreach

Our Impact

Through participation in this team, high school students gain real-world experience in many fields of interest: not only can they learn engineering and technical skills, but also critical nontechnical skills which can be directly applied to whatever future career they pursue.
