MacArthur STEM Night February 5th

Join us at MacArthur STEM Night, held at MacArthur Elementary School, where our robotics team will present an exciting robot demonstration, allowing students to drive it for themselves and share information about our team and programs. This event is a wonderful chance for students to explore STEM through interactive demos and activities!

Patrick Henry STEM Night February 5th

Join us at Patrick Henry STEM Night, where our robotics team will share information about our programs, present a live robot demonstration, where students will have the opportunity to drive the robot and learn firsthand how it works. It’s a great chance to explore STEM fields, understand robotics, and see how these technologies are shaping the future!
