2024 Summer Camps with Titan Robotics

Enrichment programs are offered in partnership with the Campagna Center's Out-of School Time Programs.

Physics FUNdamentals

Grades K-2

Registration Coming Soon

July 22-26 | 9AM-12PM

Have a blast learning the science behind explosions, collisions, and catapults while launching projectiles and (safely) blowing things up! This camp is a fun introduction to Newton's Laws and physics, as well as some awesome chemical reactions!

Introduction to Engineering and Robotics

Grades K-2

Registration Coming Soon

July 22-26 | 1PM-4PM

Campers will learn the basics of the Engineering design process through project-based learning! Campers will learn through mini-projects and challenges how to design, innovate, and create new things! The week starts as a gentle introduction to fundamental engineering principles and ends with students creating their own LEGO robots!

LEGO Mindstorms

Grades 3-5

Registration Coming Soon

July 8-12 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will construct their own robots out of LEGO! Throughout the week they will compete against each other in a robotics competition, to see who can create the best LEGO robot!

Rocketry

Grades 3-5

Registration Coming Soon

July 8-12 | 1PM-4PM

Campers will learn the basics of aerodynamics and rocket physics! Then, they will construct and launch two of their own model rockets! They will learn about the history of rocketry, the chemistry behind rocket engines, and the strategic skills needed to put a person in space! Parents are, of course, welcome to attend the rocket launch, which will be on July 12th.

Java Programming

Grades 6-9

Application Coming Soon

July 15-19 | 9AM-12PM

Learn the inner workings behind popular video games and modern robots! Java is a common programming language with countless applications. Campers will be taught many advanced aspects of Java code. Throughout the week, they will complete mini-projects, as well as working on mini robots.

Mechanical Masters

Grades 6-9

Application Coming Soon

July 15-19 | 1PM-4PM

Campers at the Mechanical Masters camp will be taught how to design, plan, and create magnificent projects! Campers will create several projects using the tools in our shop, including their own toolboxes, catapults, wooden mechanisms.

Health Sciences

Grades 6-9

Application Coming Soon

July 29 - August 2 | 9AM-12 PM

Discover the mysteries of life with a medical-based twist! Students will love our Health Sciences Camp, offered in collaboration with AIM-AHEAD! Using augmented reality technology, students will be able to see holographic projections of vital functions of the body, observe how organs function, learn the structure of human skeletons, receive hands-on experience with the science of life, and so much more!

Enrichment programs are offered in partnership with the Campagna Center's Out-of School Time Programs.
