2022 Summer Camps with Titan Robotics

We are excited to be in-person this summer for all camps! See our offerings below!

Introduction to Java Programming

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 25-29 | 9AM-12PM

Go from zero to hero in Java programming! Campers will progress from the basics of Java programming to a final project of their choosing, incorporating the skills they learned throughout the week. Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

Mechanical Masters

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 25-29 | 1PM-4PM

No one is too cool for tools! Come join Titan Robotics and take a deep dive into woodworking and 3D printing, creating lots of fun projects throughout the week. Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

Intro to Engineering and Robotics

Recommended for grades K-2

August 1-5 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will work together to discover the basics of robotics and engineering through mini projects and exploration! Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

Physics FUNdamentals

Recommended for grades K-2

August 1-5 | 1PM-4PM

Have a blast learning the science behind explosions, collisions, and catapults while launching projectiles and (safely) blowing things up! This camp is a fun introduction to Newton's Laws and physics. Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

LEGO Mindstorms

Recommended for grades 3-5

August 8-12 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will work in teams to design, build, and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges through utilization of the engineering design process. Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

Rocketry

Recommended for grades 3-5

August 8-12 | 1PM-4PM

Titan Robotics Rocketry camp is out of this world! Campers will have the opportunity to learn about the science of rockets and space travel through interactive lessons, then design and build TWO of their own rockets which they will launch at the end of the week. Register here by June 1 to secure your spot!

All camps are $215, and are hosted at Alexandria City High School.
