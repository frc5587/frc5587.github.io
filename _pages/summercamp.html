2022 Summer Camps with Titan Robotics

We are excited to be in-person this summer for all camps! See our offerings below!

Introduction to Java Programming

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 25-29 | 9AM-12PM

Learn the inner workings behind popular video games and computer programs! Java is a common programming language that has many applications. Campers will be taught many advanced aspects of Java code. At the end of the week, they will apply this gained knowledge to a final project. Register here by June 1!

Mechanical Masters

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 25-29 | 1PM-4PM

Campers at the Mechanical Masters camp will be taught how to design, plan, and create magnificent projects! Campers will create multiple projects using the tools in our shop, each one different from the others. Register here by June 1!

Intro to Engineering and Robotics

Recommended for grades K-2

August 1-5 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will work together to discover the basics of robotics and engineering through mini projects and exploration! Register here by June 1!

Physics FUNdamentals

Recommended for grades K-2

August 1-5 | 1PM-4PM

Have a blast learning the science behind explosions, collisions, and catapults while launching projectiles and (safely) blowing things up! This camp is a fun introduction to Newton's Laws and physics. Register here by June 1!

LEGO Mindstorms

Recommended for grades 3-5

August 8-12 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will work in teams to design, build, and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges through utilization of the engineering design process. Register here by June 1!

Rocketry

Recommended for grades 3-5

August 8-12 | 1PM-4PM

Campers will be taught how rocket propulsion works, and construct two rockets of their own. They will learn about both historical and modern rocket designs, and design a model rocket to fly high into the sky. about the important concepts of rocketry throughout the camp, and apply them to your final project! Register here by June 1!

All camps are $230, and are hosted at Alexandria City High School.
