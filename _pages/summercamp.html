2024 Summer Camps with Titan Robotics

Enrichment programs are offered in partnership with the Campagna Center's Out-of School Time Programs.

We are excited to once again be able to offer camps for grades 6-9 for no cost thanks to a generous grant from AIM-AHEAD!

*Please note that these dates are awaiting confirmation from ACPS building admin. While they are unlikely to change, it is possible.

Explosions, Collisions, and Catapults!

Grades K-2

$230

July 21-25* | 9AM-12PM

Have a blast learning the science behind explosions, collisions, and catapults while launching projectiles and (safely) blowing things up! This camp is a fun introduction to Newton's Laws and physics, as well as some awesome chemical reactions! Apply here!

Introduction to Engineering and Robotics

Grades K-2

$230

July 21-25* | 1PM-4PM

Campers will learn the basics of the Engineering design process through project-based learning! Campers will learn through mini-projects and challenges how to design, innovate, and create new things! The week starts as a gentle introduction to fundamental engineering principles and ends with students creating their own LEGO robots! Apply here!

LEGO Robotics

Grades 3-5

$230

July 7-11* | 9AM-12PM

Campers will construct their own robots out of LEGO! Throughout the week they will compete against each other in a robotics competition, to see who can create the best LEGO robot! Apply here!

Rocketry

Grades 3-5

$230

July 7-11* | 1PM-4PM

Campers will learn the basics of aerodynamics and rocket physics! Then, they will construct and launch two of their own model rockets! They will learn about the history of rocketry, the chemistry behind rocket engines, and the strategic skills needed to put a person in space! Parents are, of course, welcome to attend the rocket launch, which will be on July 12th. Apply here!

Intro to Java Programming

Grades 6-9

Scholarship Only

July 14-18 & July 28-August 1* | 9AM-12PM

Learn the inner workings behind popular video games and modern robots! Java is a common programming language with countless applications. Campers will be taught many advanced aspects of Java code. Throughout the week, they will complete mini-projects, as well as working on mini robots. Apply here!

Mechanical Masters

Grades 6-9

Scholarship Only

July 14-18 & July 28-August 1* | 1PM-4PM

Campers at the Mechanical Masters camp will be taught how to design, plan, and create magnificent projects! Campers will create several projects using the tools in our shop, including their own toolboxes, catapults, wooden mechanisms. Apply here!
