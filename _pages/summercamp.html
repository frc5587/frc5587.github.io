2023 Summer Camps with Titan Robotics

REGISTRATION OPENS FEBRUARY 18

Physics FUNdamentals

Recommended for grades K-2

July 24-28 | 9AM-12PM

Have a blast learning the science behind explosions, collisions, and catapults while launching projectiles and (safely) blowing things up! This camp is a fun introduction to Newton's Laws and physics. Register here!

Intro to Engineering and Robotics

Recommended for grades K-2

July 24-28 | 1PM-4PM

Campers will work together to discover the basics of robotics and engineering through mini projects and exploration! Register here!

LEGO Mindstorms

Recommended for grades 3-5

July 10-14 | 9AM-12PM

Campers will work in teams to design, build, and program LEGO Mindstorms robots to compete in fun challenges through utilization of the engineering design process. Register here!

Rocketry

Recommended for grades 3-5

July 10-14 | 1PM-4PM

Campers will be taught how rocket propulsion works, and construct two rockets of their own. They will learn about both historical and modern rocket designs, and design a model rocket to fly high into the sky. about the important concepts of rocketry throughout the camp, and apply them to your final project! Register here!

Introduction to Java Programming

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 17-21 | 9AM-4PM

Learn the inner workings behind popular video games and modern robots! Java is a common programming language that has many applications. Campers will be taught many advanced aspects of Java code. Throughout the week, they will complete miniprojects, as well as working on mini robots. Register here!

Mechanical Masters

Recommended for grades 6-9

July 31-August 4 | 9AM-4PM

Campers at the Mechanical Masters camp will be taught how to design, plan, and create magnificent projects! Campers will create multiple projects using the tools in our shop, each one different from the others. Register here!

All camps are of $230, and are hosted at Alexandria City High School.
